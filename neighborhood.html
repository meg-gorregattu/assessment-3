About Maple Grove

I live in the city of Maple Grove, Minnesota. There are plenty of fun things to around here including shopping, restaurants, and parks! Read below for more information.

Shopping


The largest shopping area in Maple Grove is The Shoppes at Arbor Lakes. You'll find a variety of shops including H&M, Hot Topic, Claire's, and even some grocery stores. Spend a summer afternoon shopping at your favorite stores and then relaxing at any of the outdoor seating areas while sipping some boba tea or iced coffee.

Restaurants


There are so many restuarants to choose from in Maple Grove. A couple of my favorites are Malone's Bar & Grill and Brick & Bourbon. Try out the restaurant generator below for some restaurant suggestions in Maple Grove.

Parks


There are plenty of parks to enjoy in Maple Grove as well. Take your dog on a walk along a trail or go swimming at one of many lakes in the Maple Grove area. I've included the most popular Maple Grove parks below:

  • Elm Creek Park Reserve
  • Fish Lake Regional Park
  • Eagle Lake Regional Park
